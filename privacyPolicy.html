Last Updated on January 21st, 2021

Jared Keffer (“I”), the developer of the application (“Rhythm Runner Revolution”), respects the data and privacy of the user (“you” or “your”). I collect your data anonymously through Unity Analytics. Unity Ads and Unity Analytics may collect additional data. Unity's Privacy Policy is located at this URL: https://unity3d.com/legal/privacy-policy

To find out specifically what data Unity collects and uses, please read these sections of Unity’s Privacy Policy under “1. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)”:

  • “I play a game built with Unity software, what should I know?”
